Design an "infographic" about a self-chosen conspiracy theory. Infographics (a clipped compound of "information" and "graphics") are graphic visual representations of information, data or knowledge intended to present information quickly and clearly. They can improve cognition by utilizing graphics to enhance the human visual system's ability to see patterns and trends. Similar pursuits are information visualization, data visualization, statistical graphics, information design, or information architecture. I chose ‘9/11 was an inside job’ as conspiracy theory. Based on exploded views I made a kind of gun made by airplane pieces.
